About The University

Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST) (Bengali: পাবনা বিজ্ঞান ও প্রযুক্তি বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়) is a government financed public university in Bangladesh.[1] PUST was established in 2008. It is the first science and Technology University and third public University in Rajshahi Division. It started its four-year undergraduate programme in 2009.This university plays an innovative role in providing need-based higher education, training, and research. It is the 7th science and technology University and 29th public University in Bangladesh
